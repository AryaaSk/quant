The Board has also determined that Ms. Jain qualifies as an “audit committee financial expert” under the criteria set forth in Item 407(d)(5) of Regulation S-K. The Board appointed Ms. Jain to serve on the Audit Committee of the Board. ​ Ms. Jain will receive the standard compensation for non-employee directors, as described in the section entitled “Non-Employee Director Compensation” in the Company’s proxy statement filed with the SEC on September 29, 2023. In addition, Ms. Jain has an indemnification agreement with the Company pursuant to which the Company will indemnify her from certain liabilities that may arise by reason of her status as a director and to advance certain expenses incurred by her.
