As previously reported in a Current Report on Form 8-K filed with the Securities and Exchange Commission (the "SEC") on November 20, 2020, on November 19, 2020 Phunware, Inc. (the "Company") received written notice from the Listing Qualifications Staff of the Nasdaq Stock Market LLC (“Nasdaq”) that the closing bid price for its common stock had been below $1.00 for the last 30 consecutive business days and that the Company therefore was not in compliance with the minimum bid price requirement for continued inclusion on the Nasdaq Capital Market under Nasdaq Listing Rule 5550(a)(2) (the “Bid Price Requirement”). On January 19, 2021, the Company received written notice from Nasdaq notifying the Company that compliance was regained under the Bid Price Requirement because the bid price of our common stock closed at or above $1.00 per share for a period of 20 consecutive business days. Per the notice, Nasdaq considers the matter closed.
