On July 26, 2024, Bradley (“Brad”) Weston provided notice of his decision to resign from the Board of Directors (the “Board”) of Vera Bradley, Inc. (the “Company”) effective August 30, 2024. Consistent with the Company's Board augmentation and refreshment standards, the Company has commenced a search for Mr. Weston’s replacement with the assistance of an experienced direct search firm. Mr. Weston noted, “ I have enjoyed working with both the Vera Bradley Board and management team. I believe the Company's strong foundation, operational execution and clear strategic implementation will serve shareholders in the future.
