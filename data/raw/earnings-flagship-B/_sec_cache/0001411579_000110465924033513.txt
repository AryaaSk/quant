Donoghue and Mark Rubenstein, each of whom are shareholders of AMC Entertainment Holdings, Inc. (“AMC”), for AMC to recover so-called “short-swing” profits under Section 16(b) of the Securities Exchange Act of 1934 (the “Exchange Act”) alleged to have been realized by defendants Antara Capital Master Fund LP, Antara Capital Fund GP LLC, Antara Capital LP, Antara Capital GP LLC, and Himanshu Gulati (collectively, the “Antara Defendants”) in connection with their purchases and sales of AMC securities. If finally approved, AMC will receive $3,300,000 from the Antara Defendants and the Antara Defendants will be released from claims of violations of Section 16(b) of the Exchange Act.
