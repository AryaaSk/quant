On March 16, 2021, Ocugen, Inc. (the “ Company ”) held a Special Meeting of Stockholders (the “ Special Meeting ”) virtually. The Company adjourned the Special Meeting to April 14, 2021, solely with respect to the voting on Item 1, the approval and adoption of an amendment to the Company’s Sixth Amended and Restated Certificate of Incorporation to increase the number of authorized shares of common stock, par value $0.01 (the “ Common Stock ”) (“ Item 1 ”), in order to provide stockholders with additional time to consider and vote on such proposal. At the reconvened Special Meeting (the “ Reconvened Meeting ”) on April 14, 2021, a total of 114,987,509 shares of the Company’s Common Stock, or 61.15% of the Common Stock outstanding as of February 11, 2021, the record date for the Special Meeting, were represented virtually or by proxy. The following is a brief description of the final voting results for each of the proposals submitted to a vote of the stockholders at the Reconvened Meeting on April 14, 2021. Item 1 — Approval and Adoption of an Amendment to the Certificate of Incorporation to Increase the Number of Authorized Shares of Common Stock from 200 million to 295 million . The stockholders approved the proposal to increase the number of authorized shares of common stock from 200 million to 295 million.
