Regulation FD Disclosure On July 28, 2020, Oportun Financial Corporation (the “Company”) issued a press release announcing the implementation of an "all-in" APR cap of 36% for all newly originated loans—nationwide. The Company anticipates that the 36% APR cap on its newly originated loans will be fully implemented by mid-August. The Company also announced changes to its legal collections practices which will be effective immediately. The Company plans to provide additional details about the announcement on its second quarter 2020 earnings call, currently scheduled for August 6, 2020.
