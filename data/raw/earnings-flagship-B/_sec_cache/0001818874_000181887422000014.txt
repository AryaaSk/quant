On February 22, 2022 , SoFi Technologies, Inc. (“SoFi”), issued a press release announcing the execution of the Agreement and Plan of Merger and Reorganization, dated as of February 19, 2022, by and among SoFi, Technisys S.A. (“Technisys”), Atom New Delaware, Inc., Atom Merger Sub Corporation and Fortis Advisors LLC, as the representative, pursuant to which, upon the terms and subject to the conditions set forth therein, SoFi will acquire Technisys. A copy of the press release is attached as Exhibit 99.1 to this Current Report on Form 8-K and is incorporated herein by reference.
