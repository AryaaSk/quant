Other Events. On July 1, 2024, Virgin Galactic Holdings, Inc. (the “Company”) received a letter from the New York Stock Exchange (the “NYSE”) stating that the Company has regained compliance with Section 802.01C of the NYSE Listed Company Manual (“Section 802.01C”), which requires listed companies to maintain an average closing price per share of at least $1.00 over a 30 consecutive trading-day period. As previously disclosed, on May 29, 2024, the Company received a letter from the NYSE, indicating that the Company was not then in compliance with Section 802.01C. On June 14, 2024, the Company completed a 1-for-20 reverse stock split, and trading on the NYSE on a split-adjusted basis commenced on June 17, 2024. As of the date of this Current Report on Form 8-K, the Company is in compliance with all NYSE continued listing standards.
