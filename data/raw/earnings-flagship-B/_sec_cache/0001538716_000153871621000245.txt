Other Events Oportun Financial Corporation (the "Company") entered into a confidential Settlement Agreement dated December 9, 2021 (the "Settlement Agreement") to resolve the previously disclosed lawsuit captioned Opportune LLP v. Oportun, Inc. and Oportun, LLC, Civil Action No. 4:18-cv-00007, before the Trademark Trial and Appeal Board . The lawsuit is described in Item 1.
