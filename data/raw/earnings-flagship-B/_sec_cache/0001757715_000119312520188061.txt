Submission of Matters to a Vote of Security Holders. On June 29, 2020, Mohawk Group Holdings, Inc. (the “Company”) held its 2020 Annual Meeting of Stockholders (the “Meeting”). At the Meeting, a total of 9,629,098 shares, or 54.2% of the Company’s common stock issued and outstanding as of May 6, 2020, the record date for the Meeting, were represented at the Meeting online or by proxy. At the Meeting, the Company’s stockholders considered one proposal, which is described in more detail in the Company’s definitive proxy statement on Schedule 14A filed with the Securities and Exchange Commission on May 15, 2020. Set forth below is a brief description of the matter voted upon at the Meeting and the voting results with respect to such matter. Proposal No. 1: To elect William Kurtz as a Class I director to serve until the Company’s 2023 Annual Meeting of Stockholders.
