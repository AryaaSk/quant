Item 8.01 Other Events. On November 15, 2021, the Company completed its initial public offering of 175,950,000 shares of its Class A common stock at a price to the public of $78.00 per share, which includes the exercise in full by the underwriters of their option to purchase from the Company an additional 22,950,000 shares of the Company’s Class A common stock. The gross proceeds to the Company from the initial public offering were $13,724,100,000 before deducting underwriting discounts and commissions and estimated offering expenses payable by the Company.
