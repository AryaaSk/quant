Harald Kroeger has resigned as a member of the Rivian Automotive, Inc. Board of Directors (the "Board"), effective December 20, 2022. Given the recent announcement of Mr. Kroeger's new roles with another company effective January 1, 2023, the Board concluded that there were now potential conflicts of interest for Mr. Kroeger. Mr. Kroeger has served on the Board since August 2022. Mr. Kroeger was a Class II director and was not a member of any committees of the Board. Following Mr. Kroeger's resignation, the Board will reduce the size of the Board to seven members.
