Regulation FD Disclosure On December 14, 2020, FS India Solar Ventures Private Limited (“FS India”), a subsidiary of First Solar, Inc. (the “Company”), and Guidance, the nodal agency of Government of Tamil Nadu, entered into a non-binding Memorandum of Understanding (“MOU”) regarding FS India’s evaluation of the State of Tamil Nadu in India as a potential location for a manufacturing facility, subject to FS India obtaining internal corporate approval and approval of the Company’s board of directors.
