(the “Company”) is making this disclosure because its sales of equity securities not registered with the United States Securities and Exchange Commission (the “SEC”) since its Quarterly Report on Form 10-Q for the period ended March 31, 2021, filed with the SEC on May 13, 2021, in the aggregate, exceeds 1% of the number of shares of common stock outstanding as of May 1, 2021. In addition, the Company issued 240,000 unregistered subordinate voting shares in resolution of a business dispute.
