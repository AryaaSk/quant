The Board of Directors (the "Board") of SoFi Technologies, Inc. (collectively, with its subsidiaries, the "Company") established a new Risk Committee of the Board (the “Risk Committee”) effective June 30, 2021. The purpose of the Risk Committee is to assist the Board in overseeing the Company's risk management framework and to ensure that the Company and its subsidiaries, including any bank subsidiary that may in the future become operational, have appropriate policies and procedures in place related to risk identification, risk governance and risk reporting, and maintain a risk control infrastructure that is consistent with the complexity of their businesses. The Board appointed Board members Steve Freiberg (Chairman), Harvey Schwartz, Tom Hutton and Magdalena Yeşil to serve as members of the Risk Committee, effective June 30, 2021. Before the establishment of the Risk Committee, the Risk Committee's functions were generally performed by the Board's Audit and Risk Committee.
