Share Repurchase Program On September 16, 2023, the Board authorized a stock repurchase program of up to $1 billion of its Class A common stock. The timing, manner, price and amount of any repurchases are determined by the Company in its discretion and depend on a variety of factors, including legal requirements, price and economic and market conditions.
