Departure of Chief Operating Officer On December 31, 2021, Sunrun Inc. (the “Company”) and Christopher Dawson entered into a Separation and General Release Agreement (the “Separation Agreement”) in connection with Mr. Dawson stepping down from the role of Chief Operating Officer on December 31, 2021 and continuing to be a non-employee advisor to the Company through April 30, 2022 (“Advisory Services Period”).
