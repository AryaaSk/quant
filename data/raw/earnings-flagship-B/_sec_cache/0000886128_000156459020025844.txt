Other Events. Board Member Payment Deferrals As previously announced in the Definitive Proxy Statement filed by FuelCell Energy, Inc. (the “Company”) on February 24, 2020, given the extraordinary level of activity of the Company’s Board of Directors (the “Board”) during fiscal 2019 as the Company worked to restructure its management team and operations and the 56 Board meetings and 27 separate Board committee meetings held during fiscal 2019, the Compensation Committee of the Board determined to provide each current non-employee Director who served as a non-employee Director as of October 31, 2019 with a one-time cash payment of $45,000. As part of the efforts by the Company to mitigate the financial and operational impacts of the COVID-19 pandemic, on May 11, 2020, the Compensation Committee of the Board and the Directors determined to defer such $45,000 payments, and such deferral is expected to be in effect for at least three months, at which time the Company and the Directors will determine whether and when it is reasonable to pay such deferred amounts (subject to the requirements of applicable tax rules on nonqualified deferred compensation).
