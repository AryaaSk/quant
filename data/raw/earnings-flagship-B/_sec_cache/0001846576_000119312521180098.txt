Item 8.01. Other Events. On June 1, 2021, the Company completed its initial public offering of 30,344,317 shares of the Company’s Class A common stock, $0.0001 par value per share (the “ Class A Common Stock ”), including the full exercise of the underwriters’ option to purchase an additional 3,957,954 shares of Class A Common Stock, at a public offering price of $22.00 per share, pursuant to the Registration Statement. The Company sold 4,636,364 shares of Class A Common Stock and Tulco, LLC sold 25,707,953 shares of Class A Common Stock. The gross proceeds to the Company from the initial public offering were $102,000,008, before deducting underwriting discounts and commissions and estimated offering expenses payable by the Company.
