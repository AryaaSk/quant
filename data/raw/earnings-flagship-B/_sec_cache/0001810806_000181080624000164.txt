Resignation of Chief Marketing Officer On June 18, 2024, Unity Software Inc. (the “ Company ”) entered into a resignation and transition agreement (the “ Agreement ”), pursuant to which Carol Carpenter will resign as the Company’s Senior Vice President and Chief Marketing Officer, effective as of June 30, 2024 (the “ Transition Date ”). Ms. Carpenter will continue as an employee and will serve as the Company’s Strategic Marketing Advisor and will assist with the transition of her responsibilities until she departs from the Company, effective December 1, 2024 (the “ Resignation Date ”). Pursuant to the Agreement, from the Transition Date until the Resignation Date (the “ Transition Period ”), Ms. Carpenter will continue to receive her base salary and her previously granted equity awards will continue to vest during the Transition Period. The Agreement also provides for a customary release of claims by Ms. Carpenter. In addition, Ms. Carpenter will be entitled to the following benefits upon the Resignation Date: • An extension of the post-termination exercise period of her vested stock options outstanding on the Resignation Date until the earliest to occur of (i) three years following the Resignation Date, (ii) the applicable expiration date of the applicable award, or (iii) such earlier date as provided or permitted under the applicable equity plan, subject to certain conditions described in the Agreement. • A lump sum cash payment in the amount of $190,000, less applicable state and federal payroll deductions, which is equal to twenty-six weeks of her base salary. • A lump sum cash payment in the amount of $261,250, less applicable state and federal payroll deductions, which is equal to a pro-rata portion of her target bonus under the cash incentive bonus plan for 2024, corresponding to her time in service in 2024. • A lump sum cash payment in the amount of approximately $17,000, less applicable state and federal payroll deductions, which is equivalent to six months’ payment for health care continuation costs. If the Company terminates Ms. Carpenter’s employment for “cause” (as defined in the Agreement) prior to the Resignation Date, Ms. Carpenter will cease to receive her base salary, and her equity awards will cease to vest on the date of such termination, and she will not be entitled to the extended post-termination exercise period nor the cash payments described above. If Ms. Carpenter resigns prior to the Resignation Date, she will cease to receive her base salary and her equity awards will cease to vest on the date of such resignation; however, she will still be entitled to the extended post-termination exercise period and the cash payments described above. A copy of the Agreement is attached as Exhibit 10.1 to this Current Report on Form 8-K. Item 9.01 Financial Statements and Exhibits. (d) Exhibits.
