Departure of Directors or Certain Officers; Election of Directors; Appointment of Certain Officers; Compensatory Arrangements of Certain Officers. On August 8, 2022, the Board of Directors (the “Board”) of Petco Health and Wellness Company, Inc. (the “Company”) appointed Justin Tichy to succeed Michael Nuzzo as the Company’s Chief Operating Officer, effective as of August 8, 2022. Mr. Tichy’s new title is Chief Pet Care Center Officer and Chief Operating Officer. Mr. Nuzzo stepped down from his current role on August 8, 2022 and will be leaving the Company, effective as of August 13, 2022. A copy of the press release announcing these and other organizational changes at the Company is attached hereto as Exhibit 99.1. Prior to his appointment as the Company’s Chief Operating Officer, Mr. Tichy, age 50, had been serving as the Company’s Chief Pet Care Center Officer since October 2018. Prior to joining the Company, Mr. Tichy served from May 2015 to October 2018 as President of Sales at Confie, one of the largest privately held insurance brokers in the United States. Previously, he held key leadership positions at Best Buy Co., Inc., Target Corp., and Walmart Inc. Mr. Tichy holds a bachelor’s degree in business management from Pennsylvania State University and a master’s degree in organizational management from the University of Phoenix. There are no family relationships that require disclosure under Item 401 of Regulation S-K or transactions involving Mr. Tichy and the Company that require disclosure under Item 404(a) of Regulation S-K, nor is Mr. Tichy a party to any arrangement or understanding with any other person pursuant to which he was selected as an officer of the Company.
