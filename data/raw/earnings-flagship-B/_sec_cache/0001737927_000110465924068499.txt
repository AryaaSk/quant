The A&R Acreage Credit Agreement also amends the financial covenants applicable to Acreage for each fiscal quarter ending December 31, 2024 and thereafter, including a minimum cash balance of US$3 million, a maximum Senior Leverage Ratio (as defined in the A&R Acreage Credit Agreement) of 4.75, a maximum Total Leverage Ratio (as defined in the A&R Acreage Credit Agreement) of 6.50 and a minimum Fixed Charge Coverage Ratio (as defined in the A&R Acreage Credit Agreement) of 1.00. There are no financial covenants of Acreage applicable until December 31, 2024.
